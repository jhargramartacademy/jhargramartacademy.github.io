Classes

Jhargram Art Academy has 3 particular divisions when it comes to classes - Painting, Dance and Art. All of the classes are available for all different age groups. We also have annual Summer Camps for everyone where variety of workshops are held from the numerous strands of art, painting and dance. There is an annual exhibition where all the students of the institute submit their paintings to showcase to the rest of the world. We also make puppet theatrical productions every once in a while, and perform across West Bengal and adjacent states in India. We also take on variety of other different projects from building sets to organizing cultural events.

Things we teach

Painting class

Painting is the most important part of Jhargram Art Academy. Painting is taught to students in various age categories to even the aged people. All kind of painting courses such as pastel color, water color, still life, acryllic on canvas, etc. are available on the basis of your skills and age. Facilities for examination are also available.

Dance class

Since 1990, this section of Jhargram Art Academy has been continuously working in the field of dance. The main form of dance taught here is Kathak along with Rabindra Nritya and Bharatnatyam. Facilities for examinations to test the students is also available.

Craft class

All the students who are part of the painting classes have crafts taught to them as well. Apart from that, there are weekly craft classes held for elders where various different handicrafts such as mask making, painting, jewellery, etc. are also taught.
