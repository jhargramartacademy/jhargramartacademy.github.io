Projects

Apart from it's regularly scheduled classes, Jhargram Art Academy also does other forms of projects, activities and events adjacent to the world of art, creativity, culture and nature. Jhargram Art Academy has been doing annual art exhibitions and festival with the paintings and craft works of all it's students while also giving a platform for all rural artisans of West Bengal to show their crafts. We have done many puppet theatrical productions and dance dramas depicting stories from bengali folklores, to tackling many societal issues, to expressing the love for nature.

Things we do

Puppet

Jhargram Art Academy has 3 particular divisions when it comes to classes - Painting, Dance and Art. All of the classes are available for all different age groups. We also have annual Summer Camps for everyone where variety of workshops are held from the numerous strands of art, painting and dance. There is an annual exhibition where all the students of the institute submit their paintings to showcase to the rest of the world. We also make puppet theatrical productions every once in a while, and perform across West Bengal and adjacent states in India. We also take on variety of other different projects from building sets to organizing cultural events.
