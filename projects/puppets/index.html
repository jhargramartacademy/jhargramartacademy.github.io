Puppets

Today for about 30 years we have been doing works in the field of puppet. The journey had started with a puppet production named Buddhiman Khorgos. In this journey, we have incorporated muppet as a part of our puppet productions to enhance the magical and awestrucking character of our productions. We have been continuously trying to provide happiness and moral teachings through our puppet show in West Bengal and in various other states of India as well. We have always tried to be innovative and new in creation and desining of puppet and other related works of the puppet productions.
